Developed by Divine Winners Company Limited. An all‑natural biostimulant for plants and a nutrient-rich supplement for animals.

Active agents enter via roots and leaves, supporting plant metabolism and nutrient absorption. It can also support beneficial soil microbes and improve overall crop vigor.

Application rates & directions

Use freshly prepared solution. Best applied early morning or late afternoon. Avoid extreme heat or heavy rain.

Plants

Seed treatment (before planting)

Dilution: 20 mL in 15 L clean water. Method: soak seeds ~2 minutes (if required) or spray/coat evenly; air‑dry in shade before planting.

Seedlings

Dilution: 50 mL in 15 L clean water. Method: foliar spray or soil drench. Frequency: every 7–14 days.

Horticultural & vegetable crops

Dilution: 100 mL in 15 L clean water (per acre). Method: foliar spray or soil drench. Frequency: every 14–21 days.

Tree crops

Dilution: 100–150 mL in 15 L clean water (per acre). Method: foliar spray or soil drench. Frequency: every 14–21 days.

Animals

Livestock (water / feed)

Water use: 5 mL per 1 L drinking water. Feed mix: 1 L per 100 kg dry feed. Frequency: daily or during stress.

Poultry (water / feed)

Water use: 2 mL per 1 L drinking water. Feed mix: 1 L per ton of feed. Frequency: 5–7 days/month or as needed.

Fish & aquaculture (pond / feed)

Pond treatment: dilute and apply every 14 days (follow label guidance). Feed additive: 5 mL per kg fish feed, with daily feeding.

Pro tips

Apply once plants have enough leaves for good absorption. If using fertilizer, apply VITAL 05 at least 7 days before fertilizer to stimulate root growth first.
