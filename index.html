Application Rates & Directions

Use freshly prepared solution. Best applied early morning or late afternoon. Avoid extreme heat or heavy rain.

Method: soak seeds ~2 minutes (if required) or spray/coat evenly; air‚Äëdry in shade before planting.

Introduction

VITAL 05 is an all-natural biostimulant concentrate produced from plant extracts through a natural fermentation process. It contains growth regulators (triacontanol, brassinosteroids), vitamins, enzymes, and essential nutrients that support plant metabolism and animal health.

Composition

Growth Regulators

Triacontanol and Brassinosteroids that promote cell division and elongation

Vitamins & Enzymes

Essential co-factors for metabolic processes and stress tolerance

Macro Nutrients

N, P, K, Ca, Mg in balanced ratios for optimal growth

Fe, Cu, Zn, Mn for enzyme activation and plant vigor

University of Ghana Lettuce Trial Report

Study Overview

This study was conducted by the Department of Crop Science, University of Ghana, to evaluate the efficacy of VITAL 05 on lettuce (Lactuca sativa) growth, yield, and quality parameters.

Experimental Design

Location: University of Ghana Research Farm
Duration: 8 weeks (full growth cycle)
Treatments: 4 different concentrations of VITAL 05 + Control
Replication: 3 times in randomized complete block design

Key Findings

Growth Increase

35-42% increase in vegetative growth compared to control

Yield Improvement

28% higher fresh weight yield at optimal concentration

Leaf Quality

Improved leaf color, thickness, and reduced tip burn

Root Development

45% more extensive root system with better nutrient uptake

Optimal Application Rate

The study concluded that 100 mL of VITAL 05 per 15L of water applied as foliar spray every 14 days provided the best cost-benefit ratio for lettuce production.

Recommendation: For commercial lettuce production, apply VITAL 05 at 100 mL/15L water starting 2 weeks after transplanting, with subsequent applications every 14 days until 1 week before harvest.
